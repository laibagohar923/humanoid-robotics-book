 # <span class="pageHeading">ABOUT</span>

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook**!

This project is designed to teach students and developers the essential concepts of **Physical AI**, **Humanoid Robotics**, and modern intelligent machines.

You will learn:

- **ROS 2** foundations  
- **Gazebo** and **Unity** simulation  
- **NVIDIA Isaac™** tools  
- Vision–Language–Action (**VLA**) systems  
- Real robotics engineering workflows  

We provide:

- Hands-on exercises  
- Step-by-step module explanations  
- A complete guided learning path from beginner to advanced robotics engineering  

Explore the fascinating world of humanoid robots at your own pace and build the skills needed for the future of robotics!
